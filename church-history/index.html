Our History

Celebrating God's faithfulness through the years.

Our Beginning

GracePath Network traces its roots to God's Grace Sanctuary Church, where Pastor David Ngure serves as a minister of the Gospel alongside other servants of God. Together, they are committed to preaching the Word of God, making disciples, nurturing believers, and transforming lives through the saving power of Jesus Christ.

What began as a local fellowship has grown into a vibrant ministry committed to worship, discipleship, evangelism, family strengthening, leadership development, and community outreach. Through God's grace, many lives have been transformed and equipped for Kingdom service.

Our Journey

2006

Pastor David Ngure assumed leadership and continued building a Christ-centered ministry founded on prayer, faith, and God's Word.

Growth & Discipleship

The ministry expanded through evangelism, prayer meetings, discipleship classes, Bible teaching, and community ministry.

Community Impact

Family ministries, youth programs, leadership development initiatives, and outreach activities helped strengthen the surrounding community.

GracePath Network

The ministry embraced the GracePath Network vision to connect people, impact lives, and advance God's Kingdom locally and globally.

Our Foundation

GracePath Network remains committed to:

  • Preaching the Gospel of Jesus Christ.
  • Making committed disciples.
  • Building strong Christian families.
  • Raising godly leaders.
  • Serving communities with love and compassion.
  • Advancing God's Kingdom locally and globally.

Looking Ahead
