Church History

Celebrating God's faithfulness through the years.

Our Beginning

God's Grace Sanctuary Naishi was started by a group of believers coming together. Pastor David Ngure took over leadership in 2006 and since then the church has been going strong. The ministry began with a passion to preach the Gospel of Jesus Christ, make disciples, and transform lives through the power of God's Word.

What started as a small fellowship has grown into a vibrant church committed to worship, prayer, discipleship, evangelism, and community outreach. Through God's grace, many lives have been touched, families restored, and believers equipped for Kingdom service.

Our Journey

2006

God's Grace Sanctuary officially established under the leadership of Pastor David Ngure.

Growth & Discipleship

The church expanded through evangelism, prayer meetings, discipleship classes and community ministry.

Community Impact

Various outreach programs, family ministries, youth programs and leadership development initiatives were launched.

Today

The ministry continues to proclaim Christ, raise disciples and impact communities for God's glory.

Our Foundation

Since its inception, God's Grace Sanctuary has remained committed to:

  • Preaching the Gospel of Jesus Christ.
  • Making committed disciples.
  • Building strong Christian families.
  • Raising godly leaders.
  • Serving the community with love and compassion.
  • Preparing believers for Christ's return.

Looking Ahead

As we continue forward, our desire is to reach more people with the Gospel, strengthen believers in their faith, support missions, and advance God's Kingdom locally and globally.
