Our History

Celebrating God's faithfulness through the years.

Our Beginning

GracePath Network traces its roots to God's Grace Sanctuary, a ministry founded through the vision of believers committed to sharing the Gospel of Jesus Christ. Under the leadership of Pastor David Ngure since 2006, the ministry has continued to grow through faithful preaching, prayer, discipleship, and community outreach.

What began as a local fellowship has developed into a growing network focused on connecting people, impacting lives, and advancing God's Kingdom. Through God's grace, many lives have been transformed, families strengthened, and leaders raised for Kingdom service.

Our Journey

2006

Pastor David Ngure assumed leadership and began building a ministry centered on Christ, discipleship, and prayer.

Growth & Discipleship

The ministry expanded through evangelism, Bible teaching, prayer meetings, discipleship classes, and community outreach.

Community Impact

Family ministries, youth programs, leadership development, and outreach initiatives were established.

GracePath Network

The ministry embraced the GracePath Network vision to connect people, impact lives, and advance God's Kingdom locally and globally.

Our Foundation

GracePath Network remains committed to:

  • Preaching the Gospel of Jesus Christ.
  • Making committed disciples.
  • Building strong Christian families.
  • Raising godly leaders.
  • Serving communities with compassion.
  • Advancing God's Kingdom locally and globally.
