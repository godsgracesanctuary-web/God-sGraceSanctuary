Our History

Celebrating God's faithfulness through the years.

Our Beginning

GracePath Network traces its roots to God's Grace Sanctuary Church, where Pastor David Ngure serves alongside other servants of God with a shared passion for preaching the Gospel of Jesus Christ, making disciples, and transforming lives through God's Word.

What began as a local fellowship has grown into a vibrant ministry committed to worship, prayer, discipleship, evangelism, and community outreach. Through God's grace, many lives have been transformed, families strengthened, and believers equipped for Kingdom service.

Our Journey

2006

The ministry continued its mission of sharing the Gospel, nurturing believers, and strengthening the local church through prayer and discipleship.

Growth & Discipleship

The ministry expanded through evangelism, Bible teaching, prayer meetings, discipleship classes, and community outreach.

Community Impact

Family ministries, youth programs, outreach initiatives, and compassionate service helped strengthen the community.

GracePath Network

The ministry embraced the GracePath Network vision to connect people, impact lives, and advance God's Kingdom locally and globally.

Our Foundation

GracePath Network remains committed to:

  • Preaching the Gospel of Jesus Christ.
  • Making committed disciples.
  • Building strong Christian families.
  • Equipping believers for ministry and service.
  • Serving communities with compassion.
  • Advancing God's Kingdom locally and globally.
