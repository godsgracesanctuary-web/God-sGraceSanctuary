About God's Grace Sanctuary

A Home of Light and Truth

Who We Are

God's Grace Sanctuary is a Christ-centered church committed to preaching the Gospel, making disciples, building strong families, raising godly leaders, and impacting communities through the love and power of Jesus Christ.

Since its establishment in 2020, the ministry has continued to grow through prayer, worship, biblical teaching, evangelism, discipleship, and community outreach.

Senior Pastor

Pastor David Ngure

Pastor David Ngure is the senior pastor of God's Grace Sanctuary Naishi. With a passion for God's Word and a heart for people, he has faithfully led the ministry since 2020, equipping believers, strengthening families, and raising leaders for Kingdom service.

Our Vision

To raise Christ-centered believers who are spiritually mature, empowered by the Holy Spirit, and transforming their generation through the Gospel of Jesus Christ.

Our Mission

  • Proclaim the Gospel of Jesus Christ.
  • Make committed disciples of Christ.
  • Build strong and godly families.
  • Develop servant leaders.
  • Serve communities through compassion and outreach.
  • Prepare believers for Christ's return.

Our History

God's Grace Sanctuary has been going strong from 2020 under Pastor David Ngure, with the assistance of a very dedicated men and women. What began as a vision from God has grown into a ministry dedicated to transforming lives through worship, prayer, discipleship, evangelism, and community outreach.

Today the church continues to impact lives while remaining committed to its calling of spreading the Gospel and advancing God's Kingdom.
