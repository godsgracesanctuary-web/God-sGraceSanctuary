Vision & Mission

Discover the purpose, calling, and direction of God's Grace Sanctuary.

Our Vision

To raise Christ-centered believers who are spiritually mature, empowered by the Holy Spirit, and transforming their generation through the Gospel of Jesus Christ.

Our Mission

God's Grace Sanctuary exists to glorify God by proclaiming the Gospel, making disciples, building strong families, equipping leaders, and demonstrating God's love through ministry and community service.

Evangelism

Reaching people with the saving message of Jesus Christ.

Discipleship

Teaching believers to grow in faith, prayer, and God's Word.

Family Ministry

Building strong marriages, families, and godly relationships.

Leadership Development

Training and equipping leaders for effective Kingdom service.

Community Outreach

Serving communities through compassion, missions, and practical ministry.

Worship & Prayer

Cultivating a lifestyle of worship, prayer, and dependence on God.

Our Core Values

  • Christ-Centered Living – Jesus Christ is the foundation of all we do.
  • Biblical Truth – God's Word is our final authority.
  • Prayer – We believe in the power of prayer.
  • Integrity – Honoring God through honesty and faithfulness.
  • Excellence – Serving God with our very best.
  • Love & Compassion – Demonstrating Christ's love to all people.
  • Discipleship – Raising mature and committed followers of Christ.

Our Scripture Foundation

"Go therefore and make disciples of all nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit."

Matthew 28:19

Looking Forward

As we move forward, we are committed to expanding God's Kingdom, raising leaders, strengthening families, reaching communities, and impacting nations with the Gospel of Jesus Christ.
