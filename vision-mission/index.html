Vision & Mission

Discover the vision, mission, and values that guide GracePath Network.

Our Vision

To connect people to Jesus Christ, nurture spiritual growth, and advance God's Kingdom by raising disciples who influence their communities and generations for God's glory.

Our Mission

GracePath Network exists to connect people with God and one another, impact lives through the transforming power of the Gospel, and advance God's Kingdom through discipleship, prayer, evangelism, worship, and compassionate service.

Connecting People

Building meaningful relationships that bring people closer to God and one another.

Impacting Lives

Sharing God's love and truth to transform lives, families, and communities.

Advancing the Kingdom

Proclaiming the Gospel and raising disciples who influence the world for Christ.

Prayer & Worship

Cultivating a lifestyle of prayer, worship, and dependence upon God.

Discipleship

Equipping believers to grow in faith, character, and spiritual maturity.

Community Outreach

Demonstrating God's love through service, compassion, and practical ministry.

Our Core Values

  • Christ-Centered Living – Jesus Christ is the foundation of everything we do.
  • Biblical Truth – God's Word guides our faith, doctrine, and daily living.
  • Prayer – We believe prayer changes lives and strengthens believers.
  • Discipleship – We are committed to spiritual growth and maturity.
  • Love & Compassion – We reflect Christ's love through service and care.
  • Integrity – We honor God through honesty, faithfulness, and accountability.
  • Excellence – We strive to serve God with our best in all things.

Our Scripture Foundation

"Go therefore and make disciples of all nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit."

Matthew 28:19

Looking Forward

As GracePath Network continues to grow, we are committed to connecting people to Christ, strengthening believers through discipleship, reaching communities with the Gospel, and advancing God's Kingdom both locally and globally.
